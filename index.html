The Meduseld Site Search

Finding the golden hall of Edoras in the West Austin Hill Country

The brief: a buildable ridge-edge homesite within ~60 min of Austin — a narrow lip where the house sits at the edge and the land falls away over a wide arc, on a green grass-and-woods mosaic. It must be a 30–500 ac private parcel that could actually be bought (not public land, not a subdivision).

Buyable edge sites (153)

Each green marker is a narrow ridge lip on a 30–500 ac private parcel, ≤60 min from Austin, undeveloped — gated by buyability, then ranked by wrap-around view + grass/woods mosaic (sheer-cliff drama down-weighted). The green outline is the real parcel (TxGIO appraisal data); the popup shows owner, acreage, and mosaic %.

These are terrain + parcel matches, not active listings. They cluster across Blanco, Hays, Comal, Kendall, Travis, Burnet, Llano & Gillespie counties. The remaining step is checking which are actually for sale — pull current listings (LandWatch / Land.com) for these owners/parcels and walk the best with a Hill Country ranch broker.
